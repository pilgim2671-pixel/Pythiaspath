About Pythia's Path

Pythia's Path is a structured decision companion designed to help you compare complex choices across multiple criteria.

Use it for career decisions, project selection, purchases, or any situation where you want your reasoning to be explicit and transparent.

Decision Maker Guide

Define options, tune criteria, and let the board compute which choice aligns best with what matters most to you.

You can adjust criteria directions (higher or lower is better), then save snapshots of your reasoning as you explore different scenarios.
