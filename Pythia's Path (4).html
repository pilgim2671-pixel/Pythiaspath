About Pythia's Path

Pythia's Path is an intelligent decision-making tool designed to help you make the best choices in your personal and professional life. Named after Pythia, the Oracle of Delphi, our application brings clarity to complex decisions.

Our Mission

We believe that every decision matters. By providing a structured, analytical approach to decision-making, we empower users to overcome analysis paralysis and choose with confidence.

Features

Core Features

Multi-Criteria Analysis: Evaluate options based on multiple weighted criteria.

Dynamic Scoring: Real-time calculation with support for both "higher is better" and "lower is better" criteria.

Custom Criteria: Add your own decision factors tailored to your specific situation.

Visual Feedback: Color-coded cards and winner highlighting make results clear at a glance.

Flexible Options: Add, remove, or modify options at any time during your analysis.

Intuitive Interface: Slider-based input with real-time value display for easy evaluation.
